Frequently Asked Questions

What is MCP?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) allows AI systems to securely connect with enterprise applications like Primavera P6 and Unifier.

Which AI clients are supported?

AISidekick supports Claude Desktop, Cursor, OpenAI integrations, and other MCP-compatible AI platforms.

How do I receive my API key?

After subscription activation, your API key and MCP endpoint details will be available inside your AISidekick dashboard.
